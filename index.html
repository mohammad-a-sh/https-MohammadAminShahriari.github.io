Mohammad Amin Shahriari

About Me

Mohammad Amin Shahriari completed his Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree in Remote Sensing at the Center for Remote Sensing and Geographic Information System Research, Faculty of Earth Sciences, Shahid Beheshti University, Tehran, Iran, in 2021. His research interests lie at the intersection of Remote Sensing, Artificial Intelligence, and Earth observation technologies. He specializes in Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR), Soil Moisture Estimation, Vegetation Parameter Estimation, Time Series Analysis, and Big Data Processing for environmental applications. He has a strong academic and practical interest in InSAR (Interferometric Synthetic Aperture Radar) and is passionate about fieldwork, data collection, and geospatial data analysis.
